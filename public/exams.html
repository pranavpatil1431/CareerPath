📝 Entrance Exams

Comprehensive guide to entrance examinations for various courses and career paths after 12th grade.

⚙️ Engineering Entrance Exams

🏆

JEE Main

Full Form: Joint Entrance Examination

Eligibility: 12th Science with PCM

Exam Pattern: Computer Based Test (CBT)

Subjects & Marks:

  • Physics: 100 marks
  • Chemistry: 100 marks
  • Mathematics: 100 marks
  • Total: 300 marks
🎯

JEE Advanced

Full Form: Joint Entrance Examination Advanced

Eligibility: JEE Main qualified

Exam Pattern: Computer Based Test

Papers:

  • Paper 1: 3 hours
  • Paper 2: 3 hours
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Maths
  • For admission to IITs
🔧

BITSAT

Full Form: Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test

Eligibility: 12th Science with PCM

Duration: 3 hours

Sections:

  • Physics: 40 questions
  • Chemistry: 40 questions
  • Mathematics: 45 questions
  • English & Logical Reasoning: 25 questions

🩺 Medical Entrance Exams

🏥

NEET

Full Form: National Eligibility cum Entrance Test

Eligibility: 12th Science with PCB

Duration: 3 hours 20 minutes

Subject Distribution:

  • Physics: 45 questions (180 marks)
  • Chemistry: 45 questions (180 marks)
  • Biology: 90 questions (360 marks)
  • Total: 720 marks
💊

AIIMS

Full Form: All Institute of Medical Science

Eligibility: 12th Science with PCB

Duration: 3.5 hours

Sections:

  • Physics: 60 marks
  • Chemistry: 60 marks
  • Biology: 60 marks
  • General Knowledge & Aptitude: 20 marks

💼 Management Entrance Exams

📊

CAT

Full Form: Common Admission Test

Eligibility: Graduate in any discipline

Duration: 2 hours

Sections:

  • Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension
  • Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning
  • Quantitative Ability
  • For IIM admissions
🎯

MAT

Full Form: Management Aptitude Test

Eligibility: Graduate in any discipline

Duration: 2.5 hours

Sections:

  • Language Comprehension: 40 questions
  • Mathematical Skills: 40 questions
  • Data Analysis & Sufficiency: 40 questions
  • Intelligence & Critical Reasoning: 40 questions

⚖️ Law Entrance Exams

🏛️

CLAT

Full Form: Common Law Admission Test

Eligibility: 12th passed for UG, Graduate for PG

Duration: 2 hours

Sections (UG):

  • English Language: 28-32 questions
  • Current Affairs & GK: 35-39 questions
  • Legal Reasoning: 35-39 questions
  • Logical Reasoning: 28-32 questions
  • Quantitative Techniques: 13-17 questions
📚

LSAT

Full Form: Law School Admission Test

Eligibility: 12th passed

Duration: 2 hours 20 minutes

Sections:

  • Analytical Reasoning
  • Logical Reasoning (2 sections)
  • Reading Comprehension
  • Unscored Variable Section

📖 Preparation Tips

📅 Time Management

  • Create a realistic study schedule
  • Allocate time for each subject
  • Include breaks and revision time
  • Practice with mock tests regularly

📚 Study Strategy

  • Understand concepts thoroughly
  • Solve previous year papers
  • Take online mock tests
  • Join study groups or coaching

🧠 Mental Preparation

  • Stay positive and motivated
  • Manage exam stress effectively
  • Get adequate sleep and exercise
  • Maintain a healthy diet
