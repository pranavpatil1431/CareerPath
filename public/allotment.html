🎯 Seat Allotment Process

Understanding the seat allocation process for various courses and institutions based on merit and preferences.

🔄 Allotment Rounds

1️⃣

First Round

Date: April 10, 2026

Time: 2:00 PM

Process:

  • Based on merit rank and preferences
  • Seats allocated to top candidates
  • Results published online
  • Document verification starts
2️⃣

Second Round

Date: April 20, 2026

Time: 2:00 PM

Process:

  • Vacant seats from Round 1
  • Up-gradation of candidates
  • Fresh allotments made
  • Choice filling for Round 3
3️⃣

Third Round

Date: April 30, 2026

Time: 2:00 PM

Process:

  • Final round of allotment
  • All remaining seats filled
  • No further rounds
  • Admission confirmation required

📊 Allotment Criteria

🏆 Merit-Based

Primary allotment based on academic performance

  • 12th Grade Marks (70%)
  • Entrance Exam Score (20%)
  • Extracurricular Activities (10%)

🎯 Preference-Based

Based on candidate's course and college preferences

  • First preference gets priority
  • Available seats in preferred course
  • College ranking and reputation

🏷️ Category-Based

Reserved seats for different categories

  • General: 50% seats
  • OBC: 27% seats
  • SC: 15% seats
  • ST: 7.5% seats
  • EWS: 10% seats

📋 Document Verification

⚠️ Important Notice

Candidates must report for document verification within 3 days of seat allotment. Failure to do so will result in cancellation of the allotted seat.

📄 Required Documents

  • Allotment Letter (Print Copy)
  • 12th Grade Mark Sheet & Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Character Certificate
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card & PAN Card
  • Passport Size Photographs (6 copies)

💳 Fee Payment Process

💰

Step 1: Fee Calculation

  • Tuition fee as per course
  • Development charges
  • Hostel fee (if applicable)
  • Caution deposit
🏦

Step 2: Payment Methods

  • Online Payment (Preferred)
  • Demand Draft
  • Bank Transfer (NEFT/RTGS)
  • Cash (at campus only)
📅

Step 3: Payment Deadline

  • Within 7 days of seat confirmation
  • Late fee applicable after deadline
  • Seat cancellation after 15 days
  • No refund for cancelled admissions
